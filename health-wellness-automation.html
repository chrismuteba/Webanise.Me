Our clients typically see a 60-70% reduction in no-show rates after implementing our automated reminder system. The system sends multi-channel reminders (SMS, email, and optional voice calls) at optimal times before appointments. Reminders include preparation instructions and easy options for rescheduling if needed, which significantly improves attendance rates.
