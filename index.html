🚀 Our Design Process

We make launching your website easy — from idea to online, we're with you every step of the way.

1

Discovery

  • We start by learning about your business, goals, and audience.
  • You'll fill out a quick brief so we can tailor your site to your brand.
2

Design & Content

  • We create a clean, modern design and organize your content for impact.
  • Don't have content? No problem — we can assist with text and image selection.
3

Build & Optimize

  • We build your site to be fast, mobile-friendly, and SEO-ready.
  • You'll be able to review everything before going live.
4

Launch & Training

  • Once approved, we launch your site and provide training so you can update it anytime.
  • We're here for ongoing support if needed.

⏱ Timeline: 1–4 weeks depending on your package and content readiness.
